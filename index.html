Why we Exist

We created thegeeksontv to help working professionals from other professions who want to get into programming but work a 9 to 5 job. The idea is to help you start a programming career while retaining your current job until you decide to switch career. Such people can leverage our BAP curriculum and video resources specially designed for them. We will also be hosting developers in our network who will address all the concerns you have regarding this adventure and will be there all through the way untill you land your first dream job as a junior developer.

Overview

BAP is an abbreviation for Becoming A Programmer. The BAP curriculum is an open source curriculum specially designed for working professionals. One thing you need to keep in mind is that the curriculum is flexible and will continue to change frequently. Our full curriculum is tracked in a source repository called git and hosted on GitHub below are a few highlights on its content.

MODULE ONE

We introduce you to programming, programming languages and their market share and the tools for programming.

MODULE TWO

Here, we get our hands dirty and do actual coding. We teach shared programming concepts using different programming languages.

MODULE THREE

You will be introduced to Software Engineering, understand the difference between software engineering and software development. You will also learn the various ways of building and managing a software project.

MODULE FOUR

We introduce you to basic Networking concepts and the fundamentals of web security.

MODULE FIVE

We introduce you to modern web development practices. You will learn about the frontend (HTML, CSS & JavaScript) and the backend using RubyOnRails.

MODULE SIX

Here, we introduce you to software architecture, design patterns, and algorithms.

MODULE SEVEN

You will be introduced to the concept of cloud computing. You will get to play with the big players in the space, the likes of Amazon, IBM, and Microsoft.

MODULE EIGHT

At this point, we put everything you have learned into building projects you can put on your résumé and showcase during a job hunt.
