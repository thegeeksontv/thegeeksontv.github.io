FAQ

Do you have any thoughts, questions or concerns? Hit the contact us button in this page footer.

How does the freemium work?

You get access to the first 3 modules in the BAP Curriculum. After successful completion, if you pass all assessment and code challenges, you will be eligible to upgrade to either the self learning or one-one-ones plan.

How often do you release new content?

As quickly as we can, be rest assured that new content will be uploaded before you complete the current content. Also on our Tech Talk Weekly tv show, we release new content every week. Our BAP curriculum lessons are released bi-weekly if the need be.

Do you offer job after completion of the curriculum?

Yes we do. However you will still have to go through the prospective company job interview process. We only help you in getting jobs that is suitable for you and work you through the entire interview process.

Can I cancel at any time?

First, we don't charge you until you go through the first 3 modules in the BAP curriculum, pass all code challenges and assessment in that module only then can you decide to upgrade to other plans. In the event that you upgrade to any of the plans, all payments made are non-refundable.

What age groups do you support?

We don't have any age restriction, anyone with interest and time in learning how to build software is welcome.

What if I get stuck on a lesson?

Use the community forum. There are lots of users, professionals and beginners alike willing to help you. If you are on the one-on-ones plan, you can reach out to your mentor.
